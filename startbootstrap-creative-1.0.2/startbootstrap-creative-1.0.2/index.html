Hamza Qureshi

Hamza Qureshi is a first-year Bachelor of Architecture student at Carnegie Mellon University.

Seven Years

Qureshi's affinity for architecture began at age 12, when over the course of a year, he created an entire city on Google SketchUp. Seven years later, four of which were spent taking studio classes at the Academy of Architecture and Contemporary Themes in North Bergen, New Jersey, he has assembled a skillset and portfolio that explores analog, digital, and hybrid media.
